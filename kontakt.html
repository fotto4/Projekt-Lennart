About Us

We, Tim, Oskar, Lennart, and Jonas, are the founders of a sports recruiting website that connects talented athletes with the best teams and training opportunities worldwide.
Each of us brings expertise from a different sport: Jonas and Oskar in football, Lennart in basketball, and Tim in American football.

We help athletes develop their skills, uncover hidden talent, and make the right connections with teams and coaches.
Our platform provides the necessary support to ensure every athlete can reach their full potential and take their career to the next level.
But who are we really?

Jonas

Jonas is a football expert with years of experience as a goalkeeper. He has worked in various youth academies and helps athletes improve their technical and tactical skills. Jonas connects promising talents with good teams and training opportunities.

Oskar

Oskar, an experienced football player, knows the demands of the game firsthand.
He supports athletes in recognizing and developing their strengths,
and helps them make the right decisions on their path to a football career.

Lennart

Lennart is our basketball expert and an experienced player.
He helps young talents improve their skills and uses his network to connect them with the right coaches and teams.
His passion for the sport motivates athletes to give their best.

Tim

Tim is a football expert, active player, and passionate about the sport.
With his experience in college and professional football, he helps athletes achieve their career goals and develop their strengths,
so they can find the right training opportunities and teams.
